🌱 SaltNet · Plant Salt-Tolerance Gene Database

A curated, literature-mined catalogue of plant salt-tolerance genes across species — with alias clustering, PubMed support, evidence strength, functional category, transport substrate, subcellular localization, and mechanistic role. Click any row for full details and an interactive co-citation network.
